gpt-pp-team is a protocol replay toolkit for the ChatGPT Team subscription flow — covering the full chain from Stripe Checkout through PayPal billing agreement to Codex OAuth token extraction. It includes a standalone hCaptcha visual solver (VLM + CLIP/OpenCV), a 12-loop self-healing daemon for unattended operation, and real empirical data on how ChatGPT's anti-fraud mechanisms behave.
